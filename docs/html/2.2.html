  • 4:53. рҹ§© Example вҖ“ : Find the derivative of $рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ)=рқ’ҷ^{3}$. вҖ“ [рҹ“·image]
    • Formula for the derivative:
      • $рқ’Ү'(рқ’ҷ)=\displaystyle \lim_{h\to 0}\dfrac{рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ+h)-рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ)}{h}$
    • Step 1: Define the functions:
      • $рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ+h)=(рқ’ҷ+h)^{3}$
      • $рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ)=рқ’ҷ^{3}$
    • Step 2: Substitution into the formula:
      • $рқ’Ү'(рқ’ҷ)=\displaystyle \lim_{h\to 0}\dfrac{(рқ’ҷ+h)^{3}-рқ’ҷ^{3}}{h}$
    • Step 3: Expand the cube:
      • $(рқ’ҷ+h)^{3}=рқ’ҷ^{3}+3рқ’ҷ^{2}h+3рқ’ҷh^{2}+h^{3}$
    • Step 4: Substitution of the expanded expression:
      • $рқ’Ү'(рқ’ҷ)=\displaystyle \lim_{h\to 0}\dfrac{(рқ’ҷ^{3}+3рқ’ҷ^{2}h+3рқ’ҷh^{2}+h^{3})-рқ’ҷ^{3}}{h}$
    • Step 5: Simplify the expression:
      • $рқ’Ү'(рқ’ҷ)=\displaystyle \lim_{h\to 0}\dfrac{3рқ’ҷ^{2}h+3рқ’ҷh^{2}+h^{3}}{h}$
    • Step 6: Factor out $h$:
      • $рқ’Ү'(рқ’ҷ)=\displaystyle \lim_{h\to 0}\dfrac{h\big(3рқ’ҷ^{2}+3рқ’ҷh+h^{2}\big)}{h}$
    • Step 7: Cancel $h$:
      • $рқ’Ү'(рқ’ҷ)=\displaystyle \lim_{h\to 0}\big(3рқ’ҷ^{2}+3рқ’ҷh+h^{2}\big)$
    • Step 8: Apply the limit as $h\to 0$:
      • $рқ’Ү'(рқ’ҷ)=3рқ’ҷ^{2}$
    • Final answer:
      • The derivative of $рқ’Ү(рқ’ҷ)=рқ’ҷ^{3}$ is $рқ’Ү'(рқ’ҷ)=3рқ’ҷ^{2}$.
